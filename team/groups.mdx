## Group types

| Type | Use for |
|------|---------|
| Department | HR, Engineering, Legal, etc. |
| Team | Cross-functional squads |
| Location | Office, region |
| Custom | Any grouping |

## Create groups

Team Management → **Groups** → Add groups. Add members (users) to each group.

## Use in targeting

Groups drive **audience targeting** for policies and boards: assign a policy to "Engineering" or "New York office" to control who sees it.
