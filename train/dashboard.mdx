## Overview

Employees see the **Dashboard** after login. It shows only **Policy Boards** that are **published** and **assigned** to them or their group. Each board displays completion status and due dates.

{/* Placeholder: Dashboard screenshot */}

## Dashboard layout

- **Header**: Company logo, notifications, profile
- **Hero card**: "X of Y required policies completed (Z%)"
- **Boards grid**: Card per assigned board with title, description, completion ratio
- **Global search**: Search policy titles and documents

## Board detail

Click a board to see its policies as cards:

- Status: Not started / In progress / Completed / Overdue
- Due date
- Quick action: **Start** or **Continue**
