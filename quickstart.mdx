## 3. Add a policy

Choose how to add a policy:

| Option | Use when |
|--------|----------|
| **From scratch** | Writing a new policy; AI can draft sections |
| **From template** | Using a pre-built legal board template |
| **File upload** | You have PDF, DOCX, or Markdown |
| **Integration** | Policy lives in Google Drive, SharePoint, Notion, Confluence, or OneDrive |

## 4. Configure & publish

- Set **risk level** (Low / Medium / High)
- Enable **acknowledgment** and choose cadence (once, yearly, monthly)
- Assign **audience** (groups: department, team, location)
- Add policy to your board and **Publish** the board

{/* Placeholder: Policy settings screenshot */}

## 5. Employees complete training

Employees see the **Dashboard** with only published Policy Boards assigned to them or their group. They complete training and sign acknowledgments. You can track progress and send reminders from the Track module.
