## Org-level dashboard

Admins see:

- **Overall completion rate**: % of required acknowledgments done
- **Breakdown**: By board, policy, department
- **Trend**: Last 30/90 days
- **Top Overdue Policies**: Widget
- **Employees at Risk**: Most overdue / lowest completion

## Policy analytics

Per policy:

- Total assigned vs trained vs signed
- Distribution by department/role
- Employee list with statuses: Not started / In progress / Completed (not signed) / Signed / Overdue
- Export: CSV, PDF

## Employee compliance view

For users with team-view permission: view one employee's required policies with status and timestamps. Quick action to send targeted reminder.
