## What is DocsOrb?

DocsOrb is a policy management platform that helps HR, LegalOps, FP&A leaders, and founders centralize policies, train employees, and track acknowledgments for compliance.

## For your team

| Role | Use DocsOrb to |
|------|----------------|
| **HR / LegalOps** | Create policies, assign boards, run acknowledgment campaigns, export audit evidence |
| **FP&A / Founders** | View compliance dashboards, ensure teams are up to date |
| **Employees** | Complete assigned policies, sign acknowledgments, reference policies anytime |
